The Best Movies According to Habibullah

My top 3 movies of all-time.

PK (Indian Hindi-language film)

  • Brief Description: "PK" is a comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani. It tells the story of an alien who lands on Earth and questions various societal norms and religious beliefs. The movie stars Aamir Khan, Anushka Sharma, and Sushant Singh Rajput in lead roles.
  • Key Points: Critically acclaimed for its thought-provoking narrative and Aamir Khan's performance. Addresses social issues with humor and satire.

Children of Paradise (French: Les Enfants du Paradis):

  • Brief Description: "Children of Paradise" is a classic French film directed by Marcel Carné. Set in 19th-century Paris, it revolves around the complicated love lives of various characters, including a beautiful courtesan and a mime artist. The movie is renowned for its rich storytelling and intricate character development.
  • Key Points: Considered one of the greatest films of all time. Known for its epic scope, stunning cinematography, and unforgettable performances.

Nasl-e-Aftab (Persian: نسل آفتاب):

  • Brief Description: "Nasl-e-Aftab" is an Iranian drama film directed by Saman Salur. It follows the story of a family living in Tehran during the 1980s, amidst the Iran-Iraq War. The film explores themes of resilience, survival, and the impact of war on ordinary people's lives.
  • Key Points: Praised for its authenticity and emotional depth. Offers a poignant portrayal of Iranian society during a tumultuous period in history.
